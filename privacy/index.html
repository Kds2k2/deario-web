Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

Short version: Deario keeps everything on your device. We don't have servers, we don't have accounts, and we never collect, sell, or share your personal information.

Information we collect

None. Deario does not collect any personal information. The content you create in the app — the people you add, their important dates, your notes, and any photos you choose — is stored only on your device using Apple's on-device database. It is never transmitted to us or to any third party.

Photos

If you add a photo to a person, Deario reads only the image you specifically pick and stores it inside the app's local database on your device. Deario does not access your wider photo library, and your photos are never uploaded anywhere.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device as local notifications. No reminder content leaves your device, and granting notification permission does not give us any information about you.

Tips and purchases

Deario offers optional tips to support development. These purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles all payment information — the developer does not receive your name, card details, or other payment data. Tips do not unlock features and involve no collection of personal data by us.

Backups

Because your data lives on your device, it may be included in the backups you make through iCloud or your computer. Those backups are controlled by you and managed by Apple under Apple's privacy terms. Deario has no access to them.

Analytics and tracking

Deario contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking software. We do not build profiles of you or your contacts, and there is no cross-app or cross-site tracking of any kind.

Children's privacy

Deario does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. No account or personal detail is required to use the app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Because Deario doesn't collect data, we don't anticipate material changes.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email deario.support@gmail.com.
